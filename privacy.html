Privacy Policy

Hokuto Messaging AI — Effective date: 2025-12-24

Hokuto Messaging AI respects your privacy.

This application is used by businesses to automatically respond to messages sent to their Facebook Pages via Messenger.

Data We Collect

  • Message content sent by users to a Facebook Page
  • User ID provided by Meta Platforms

How We Use Data

  • To generate automated replies to customer inquiries
  • To provide customer support services on behalf of businesses

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Data is only processed to provide messaging responses for the businesses that use the application.

Data Retention

Message data may be stored temporarily for processing and debugging. Data is not retained longer than necessary to fulfill those purposes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: hokutolee@gmail.com
